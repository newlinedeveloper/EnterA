Top 10 tips to be a successful tourism company online
Have a good website
  1. have a good and attractive design
  2. have informative, complete and attractive content
  3. be search engine optimised (SEO)
  4. be easy to update and maintain
  5. be mobile-friendly
  6. have good quality photos and videos
Offer online booking and payment:
  1. card payment
  2. bank transfer
  3. mobile payment
  4. international alternatives, such as PayPal
  5. national alternatives.
Consider selling your product through an online travel agency:
  1. massive exposure for your tourism product, leading to more bookings and revenue
  2. relatively low costs, as you do not have to invest in your own booking system
  3. European travellers like to compare offerings, so your quality product stands out
  4. good customer reviews on OTA websites may convince tourists to book your tourism product.
Be present and active on social media
  1. Instagram – to share photos and videos of your destination
  2. Facebook – to create, promote and share content and interact with (potential) customers
  3. Twitter – for real-time interaction, ranging from customer service to updates on events
  4. YouTube – to showcase your destination in video format.
Let your satisfied customers speak for you :

As we just discussed, what others say about you is your most powerful marketing message. You need to create positive word of mouth. For this, of course you have to give your customers a great experience during their holiday. In addition, you have to encourage them to share their great experience online, for example via.

  1. review sites – reviews and ratings
  2. social media – updates, photos and videos
  3. your website – comments and feedback.
Respond quickly to online customer contact :

If a (potential) customer contacts you via your website or social media, you should respond quickly. With existing customers, slow replies may lead to annoyance, bad reviews or problems if they need assistance. With potential new customers, slow replies may cost you their business.

Advertise :
  1. Google Adwords – a pay-per-click advertising service, based on search engine keywords
  2. Facebook Ads – various types of advertisements on Facebook platforms.
  3. To get started with Google Adwords, TrekkSoft gives you 10 tips for tour and activity companies to create a successful AdWords strategy.
  4. Facebook now has a guide to Facebook for Travel, with useful advertisement tools for the travel industry.
Build relationships:
  1. It is easier to sell to an existing customer than to acquire a new one. This means you should cherish your guests and make them into return customers.
  2. Good online ways to build and maintain a relationship with your guests are:
  3. staying in touch after the trip is done – travellers appreciate a personal touch
  4. asking for feedback and using it to improve your product – let your guest know how you used their feedback
  5. building an e-mail database – use your website or blog to add to your subscriber list and create effective and exclusive information for this group.
9. Collaborate with bloggers, news and/or travel writers and stakeholders in your region : Travel bloggers and writers

Travel blogging has become a key way to generate sales for your tourism products. However, you should not write all the blog posts yourself. To create trustworthy content, collaborate with travel bloggers.
